### Boundaries

- **Work stop time:** 18:00 — protect family time after this
- **Meal cutoff:** 21:00 — no eating after this (weight loss goal)
- **Late-night work:** Avoid. This is the root cause of late meals and poor sleep
- **Deep work minimum:** At least 2h of uninterrupted focus per day on #1 priority

## Energy Patterns

- **Peak energy:** Morning (08:00-10:00)
- **Post-lunch dip:** 13:00-14:00 — schedule light tasks here
- **Second wind:** 15:00-17:00
- **After meetings:** Energy drops — schedule buffer or light tasks, not deep work

## Planning Preferences

- **Planning style:** Concise, scannable. Don't over-explain goals I already know
- **Calendar blocks:** Ask before creating. Use emoji prefixes (🎯 priority, 🏋️ gym, 🍽️ lunch)
- **Recurring plans:** Keep it snappy after the first day. "Same priorities — here's today:" is fine
- **Admin tasks:** Batch together. Don't scatter across the day
- **Quick wins first:** If there's a 5-min task (like sending an email), do it before deep work so it doesn't nag

## Reflection Preferences

- **Check-in tone:** Shoulder tap, not lecture. 3-5 sentences max
- **Daily review:** Honest but warm. Acknowledge effort before pointing out drift
- **Weekly review:** Be direct about stalled goals. Ask the uncomfortable question
- **Language:** English for reflections. Japanese for communications with Japanese companies

## Coaching Style

- **What works:** Specific, actionable suggestions. "Outline Module 3" not "work on course"
- **What doesn't work:** Generic motivational talk. Overly detailed breakdowns of things I already understand
- **Procrastination approach:** Ask "what's making this hard?" — usually the task feels too big or too vague
- **Celebrate specifically:** Connect wins to the larger story, not just "good job"

## Recurring Commitments

- **Family dinner:** Every evening. Non-negotiable
- **Friend outreach:** At least 1 friend/week, proactive contact
- **Wife gifts:** Monthly small gesture minimum. Set reminders
